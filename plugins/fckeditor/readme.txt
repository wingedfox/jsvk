VirtualKeyboard plugin for FCKEditor.

INSTALLATION:
 1. Extract contents of this archive to the /plugins/ folder under FCKEditor root
 2. add line
      FCKConfig.Plugins.Add('Jsvk');
    to your fckconfig.js
 3. Update your toolbar by adding item
      'Jsvk'
    to one of the button groups


See "_sample/sample.config.js" for details.
